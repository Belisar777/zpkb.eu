Personal Data Protection Policy

This document provides information to an applicant for the provision of a social service within the meaning of Article 14 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC (GDPR).

The data controller is Plzeň Region, Company ID: 70890366, Škroupova 1760/18, 301 00 Plzeň (hereinafter also referred to as the “Controller”), tel.: +420 377 195 111, e-mail: posta@plzensky-kraj.cz, Data Box ID: zzjbr3p.

The data processor is the provider of social services to whom you have submitted an application for the provision of a social service (hereinafter also referred to as the “Processor”).

This information does not apply to the processing of personal data carried out by the social service provider for the purpose of processing the application for the provision of a social service, even though some of the data may be the same; see points 6 and 7 below for further details.

The data subject is a natural person – an applicant for a social service (hereinafter also referred to as the “Data Subject”).

The Processor transfers the following personal data of the Data Subject to the Controller: first name and surname, date of birth, municipality of permanent residence, the fact that the Data Subject has applied for the provision of a social service, or the fact that the Data Subject no longer requests the provision of a social service.

The Processor transfers personal data to the Controller by entering them into the web application “Register of Applications for the Provision of Residential Social Services” for the following purpose: obtaining data necessary to determine the need for the provision of social services to individuals or groups of persons and ensuring the availability of social services within the framework of planning the basic network of social services in the territory of the Plzeň Region within the meaning of Section 95 of Act No. 108/2006 Coll., on Social Services, as amended, according to which the region:

  • pursuant to Section 95(a) determines the needs for the provision of social services to individuals or groups of persons in its territory;

  • pursuant to Section 95(d) prepares a medium-term plan for the development of social services, which under Section 3(h) is the result of actively identifying the needs of persons in the territory of the municipality or region;

  • pursuant to Section 95(g) ensures the availability of social services in its territory in accordance with the medium-term development plan for social services;

  • pursuant to Section 95(h) determines the network of social services within the region, which under Section 3(i) is defined as a set of social services with sufficient capacity, etc.

At the same time, pursuant to Section 101a(2) of the Act, the region decides on the provision of financial resources from subsidies to social service providers to fulfil the above-mentioned objectives.

Personal data are processed on the legal basis pursuant to Article 6(1)(e) GDPR – processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in the Controller.

Plzeň Region does not transfer the personal data of the Data Subject to third countries or international organisations outside the EU.

Personal data of the applicant for a social service are processed in the register until the processing of their last application for the provision of a social service in the Plzeň Region has been completed. After that, the personal data are deleted from the register.

The Data Subject has the right to request access to their personal data from Plzeň Region, their rectification, or restriction of processing, and to object to the processing. Requests will always be properly assessed and handled in accordance with the relevant provisions of the GDPR.

The Data Subject has the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority, which is the Office for Personal Data Protection, Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Prague 7, if they believe that Plzeň Region processes their personal data in violation of the GDPR.

When processing the personal data of the Data Subject, Plzeň Region does not carry out automated decision-making or processing, including profiling, on the basis of which actions or decisions would be taken that would interfere with the rights or legitimate interests of the Data Subject.

The Data Subject may exercise their rights listed in point 11 through the Data Protection Officer of the Plzeň Region in the manner published on the website of the Plzeň Region. Contact details of the Data Protection Officer:
