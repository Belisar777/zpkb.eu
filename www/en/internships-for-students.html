Internships for students

Students and graduates, are you interested in doing an internship with us? Read on to find out how our internship works and how to apply.

At the Representation of the Pilsen Region in Brussels, both students and recent university graduates can undertake an internship. We have been offering these internships since 2008, and to date, we have welcomed over sixty interns. The following lines are intended primarily for Czech students; therefore, we do not provide additional information in English here.
