Plzeňský dům v Bruselu - Zastoupení Plzeňského kraje v EU

The Representation of the Pilsen Region in Brussels serves as a link between the region and the institutions of the European Union. Its role is to monitor developments within European institutions, identify opportunities for regional development, and provide up-to-date information on EU policies and programmes back to the Pilsen Region. The office also helps establish contacts with European partners, supports cooperation in areas such as regional development, innovation, culture, and education, and promotes the Pilsen Region at the European level.
