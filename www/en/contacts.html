Due to participation in various conferences and business meetings, or if the representative is in the Pilsen Region (usually one week per month), it may happen that no one will be available during an unannounced visit to the office (there is no permanent reception service).

Where to find us and how to get there:

The Pilsen Region Office is located at Place de Jamblinne de Meux 31, Brussels – Schaerbeek, B-1030. The building is close to the headquarters of the European Commission and the Council of the EU at Rond Point Schuman and not far from the European Parliament on Rue Wiertz and the Committee of the Regions on Rue Belliard.

From the airport, the best way to get there is by express bus no. 12 (Meiser or Schuman stop) or no. 21 (stop almost in front of the building –  Jamblinne de Meux).

By metro, the nearest station is Schuman on lines 1 and 5, then about a 10-minute walk or a 2-stop bus ride on bus no. 21 to the Jamblinne de Meux stop.
