The Permanent Representation of the Pilsen Region in Brussels is headed by Mgr. Zbyněk Prokop.
 

Would you like to visit us?

Office hours are from Monday to Friday: 9:00–12:00, 13:00–17:00.

If you plan to visit the Pilsen Region Office in Brussels, we recommend arranging an appointment in advance. Due to attendance at various conferences, working meetings, or travel to the Pilsen Region (usually one week per month), it may happen that no one is present in the office during an unannounced visit, as there is no permanent reception service.

 

Where can you find us?

The Office of the Pilsen Region is located at Place de Jamblinne de Meux 31, Brussels – Schaerbeek, B-1030. The building is close to the headquarters of the European Commission and the Council of the EU at Rond-Point Schuman, and not far from the European Parliament on Rue Wiertz and the Committee of the Regions on Rue Belliard.

From the airport, the easiest way is to take express bus No. 12 (stop Meiser or Schuman) or bus No. 21 (the stop Jamblinne de Meux is almost directly in front of the building).

The nearest metro station is Schuman on lines 1 and 5. From there it is about a 10-minute walk, or you can take bus No. 21 for two stops to Jamblinne de Meux.
